Treat your self with:

Pedicure is a cosmetic treatment for the feet, which typically involves the trimming and shaping of toenails, removal of calluses and dead skin, moisturizing and sometimes polishing of nails. It is a way to pamper and improve the appearance of feet.

Gel overlay nails is a technique of applying a thin layer of hard gel on top of natural nails to enhance strength and durability, and for an aesthetic look. It is cured under UV light and can last for several weeks.

Nail extensions are a method of enhancing the length of natural nails by attaching artificial nails or nail tips to them, typically using glue or a special adhesive. They can be made of various materials such as acrylic, gel, or silk.

Nail art is the practice of decorating nails with designs, patterns, or colors. It can include painting, drawing, stamping, or attaching small items such as rhinestones or glitter. Nail art can be done on natural nails or on top of extensions, and can be applied using various techniques such as airbrush, brush painting or stencils.
